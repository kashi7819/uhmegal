About BabyBoom Chats

BabyBoom Chats is a fast, simple and clean random video chatting platform that helps you connect with strangers across the world instantly.

Our goal is to make chatting safe, smooth, and fun for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to build a platform that lets people communicate freely while ensuring privacy and safety. BabyBoom Chats is designed to load fast, work smoothly, and create meaningful random connections.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Instant video & text chat
  • Gender preference filters
  • Clean and distraction-free UI
  • No recordings — full privacy
  • Mobile-friendly design

Safety First

We do not save video/audio/chat data. Users are responsible for following safety rules and avoiding sharing personal information.
